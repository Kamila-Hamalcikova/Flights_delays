When to take a flight to avoid delays?¶

by Kamila Hamalcikova¶

Investigation overview¶

In this investigation, I looked at different departure times of flights to predict if the flight has high chance of being delayed 15 minutes or more. The main focus was on different hour of day and different day of week.

Dataset overview¶

The data consisted of almost 2 400 thousand different flights and their various attributes (departure time, delay in minutes, airport, airline etc.). I have removed over 47 thousand observations which were related to cancelled or diverted flights.

Distribution of delays in minutes¶

Suprising fact is that almost 58 % of planes arrived earlier than their scheduled arrival. 2 % of planes arrived exactly on time and 40 % with some delay. I will focus only on significant delays with duration 15 minutes and more. These big delays happen in 22 % of fligths. Distribution of delays in general is unimodal with peak around 0, proving that most planes arrive more or less on time.

Distribution of flights during hour of day¶

We can see that airports are the most busy from 6:00 to 18:00, especially from 06:00 to 08:59. You might assume these hours would gain more delays because of heavier workload for airport employees. We will see the answer little bit later.

Also it is worth to point out that from 00:00 to 04:59 there is very little number of flights when comparing to daily busy hours later.

Chance of big delay vs. hour of departure (absolute frequency)¶

More busy time of departure doesn't equal more chances for delay on arrival of the plane. Actually, the most busy departure interval (from 06:00 to 08:59) does not result in more delays on landing. Delays tend to increase volume from morning till they reach their maximum in absolute numbers between 17:00 to 20:00 and then start to decrease.

Chance of big delay on day of week and hour of day¶

If you want to avoid delays, the worst time to take plane is from 18:00 till 20:59 on Monday or Sunday. The best time would be from 05:00 till 6:59 on any day, but especially Friday or Thursday.

Average length of delay on day of week and hour of day¶

If you dread long delays, best time to take flight is from 05:00 till 6:59 on Wednesdays or Fridays. These delays usually take not more than 10 to 15 minutes. On the other hand, the longest delays a passanger will have to endure happen usually from 17:00 to 20:59 on Monday, from 18:00 till 19:59 on Wednesday and from 18:00 till 20:59 on Sunday. They last little over 30 minutes on average.
